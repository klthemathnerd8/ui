== Problem 6 ==
For positive numbers $x$ and $y$ the operation $\spadesuit (x,y)$ is defined as 

$$\spadesuit (x,y) = x-\dfrac{1}{y}$$

What is $\spadesuit (2,\spadesuit (2,2))$?

$
\mathrm{(A)}\ \dfrac{2}{3}
\qquad
\mathrm{(B)}\ 1
\qquad
\mathrm{(C)}\ \dfrac{4}{3}
\qquad
\mathrm{(D)}\ \dfrac{5}{3}
\qquad
\mathrm{(E)}\ 2
$
