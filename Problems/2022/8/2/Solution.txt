==Solution==
We can substitute $5$, $3$, and $6$ into the functions' definitions:
$$\begin{align*}
(5 \, \blacklozenge \, 3) \, \bigstar \, 6 &= \left(5^2-3^2\right) \, \bigstar \, 6 \\
(5 \, \blacklozenge \, 3) \, \bigstar \, 6 &= \left(25-9\right) \, \bigstar \, 6 \\
&= 16 \, \bigstar \, 6 \\
&= (16-6)^2 \\
&= \boxed{\textbf{(D) } 100}.
\end{align*}$$
