==Solution (Casework)==

First, note that $1+7+5+2+5=20$. There are $3$ possible cases:

'''Case 1: the mean is $5$.'''

$X = 5 \cdot 6 - 20 = 10$.

'''Case 2: the mean is $7$.'''

$X = 7 \cdot 6 - 20 = 22$.

'''Case 3: the mean is $X$.'''

$X= \frac{20+X}{6} \Rightarrow X=4$.

Therefore, the answer is $10+22+4=\boxed{\textbf{(D) }36}$.
