== Solution ==

We compute:

$$
\begin{align*}
1 + \frac {1}{1 + \frac {1}{1 + 1}}
&=
1 + \frac {1}{1 + \frac {1}{1 + 1}}
\\
&=
1 + \frac {1}{1 + \frac 12}
\\
&=
1 + \frac {1}{\frac 32}
\\
&=
1 + \frac 23
\\
&=
\frac 53
\end{align*}
$$

This is choice $\boxed{\text{C}}$.

Interesting sidenote: The continued fraction $1 + \frac {1}{1 + \frac {1}{1 + 1....}}$ is equal to the golden ratio, or $\frac{1+\sqrt{5}}{2}$.
