== Solution ==
Suppose $x$ is the price of an adult ticket. The price of a child ticket would be $\frac{x}{2}$.

$$\begin{eqnarray*}
5x + 4(x/2) = 7x &=& 24.50\\
x &=& 3.50\\
\end{eqnarray*}$$

Plug in for 8 adult tickets and 6 child tickets.

$$\begin{eqnarray*}
8x + 6(x/2) &=& 8(3.50) + 3(3.50)\\ 
&=&\boxed{\textbf{(B)}\ \ 38.50}\\
\end{eqnarray*}$$
