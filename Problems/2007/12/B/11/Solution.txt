==Solution==

The sum of the interior angles of any quadrilateral is $360^\circ.$
$$\begin{align*}
360 &= \angle A + \angle B + \angle C + \angle D\\
&= \angle A + \frac{1}{2}A + \frac{1}{3}A + \frac{1}{4}A\\
&= \frac{12}{12}A + \frac{6}{12}A + \frac{4}{12}A + \frac{3}{12}A\\
&= \frac{25}{12}A
\end{align*}$$
$$\angle A = 360 \cdot \frac{12}{25} = 172.8 \approx \boxed{\mathrm{(D) \ } 173}$$
