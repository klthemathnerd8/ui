==Solution 1== 

We see that $\frac{44}{11}$ is $4$; 
$\frac{110}{44}$ simplifies to $\frac{5}{2}$, which is $2.5$; 

and $\frac{44}{1100}$ simplifies to $\frac{1}{25}$, which is $0.04$; 

$4+2.5+0.04$ reveals $$\frac{44}{11} + \frac{110}{44} + \frac{44}{1100}$$ is $\boxed{\textbf{(C)\ 6.54}}$.
