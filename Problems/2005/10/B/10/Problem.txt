== Problem ==
In $\triangle ABC$, we have $AC=BC=7$ and $AB=2$. Suppose that $D$ is a point on line $AB$ such that $B$ lies between $A$ and $D$ and $CD=8$. What is $BD$?

$
\textbf{(A) }\ 3      \qquad
\textbf{(B) }\ 2\sqrt{3}      \qquad
\textbf{(C) }\ 4      \qquad
\textbf{(D) }\ 5      \qquad
\textbf{(E) }\ 4\sqrt{2}
$
