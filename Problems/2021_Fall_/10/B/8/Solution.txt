== Solution==
We have
$$\begin{align*}
16383 & = 2^{14} - 1 \\
& = \left( 2^7 + 1 \right) \left( 2^7 - 1 \right) \\
& = 129 \cdot 127 \\
\end{align*}$$

Since $129$ is composite, $127$ is the largest prime which can divide $16383$. The sum of $127$'s digits is $1+2+7=\boxed{\textbf{(C) }10}$.
