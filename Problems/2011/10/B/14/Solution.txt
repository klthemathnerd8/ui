== Solution ==

Let the sides of the rectangular parking lot be $a$ and $b$. Then $a^2 + b^2 = 625$ and $ab = 168$. Add the two equations together, then factor.
$$\begin{align*}
a^2 + 2ab + b^2 &= 625 + 168 \times 2\\
(a + b)^2 &= 961\\
a + b &= 31
\end{align*}$$
The perimeter of a rectangle is $2 (a + b) = 2 (31) = \boxed{\textbf{(C)} 62}$
