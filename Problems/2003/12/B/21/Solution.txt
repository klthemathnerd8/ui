== Solution 1 (Trigonometry) ==
By the [[Law of Cosines]],
$$\begin{align*}
AB^2 + BC^2 - 2 AB \cdot BC \cos \alpha = 89 - 80 \cos \alpha = AC^2 &< 49\\
\cos \alpha &> \frac 12\\
\end{align*}$$

It follows that $0 < \alpha < \frac {\pi}3$, and the probability is $\frac{\pi/3}{\pi} = \frac 13 \Rightarrow \mathrm{(D)}$.
