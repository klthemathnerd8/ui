== Problem 10 ==
Find the number of positive integers <math>n</math> less than <math>1000</math> for which there exists a positive real number <math>x</math> such that <math>n=x\lfloor x \rfloor</math>.

Note: <math>\lfloor x \rfloor</math> is the greatest integer less than or equal to <math>x</math>.
