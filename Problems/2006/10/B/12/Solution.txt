== Solution 1 ==
Since $(1,2)$ is a solution to both equations, plugging in $x=1$ and $y=2$ will give the values of $a$ and $b$. 

$ 1 = \frac{1}{4} \cdot 2 + a $ 

$ a = \frac{1}{2} $

$ 2 = \frac{1}{4} \cdot 1 + b $

$ b = \frac{7}{4} $

So $ a+b = \frac{1}{2} + \frac{7}{4} = \boxed{\textbf{(E) }\frac{9}{4}}$.
