==Solution 1 (Factor)==
We have 
$$\begin{align*}
2021_b - 221_b &= (2021_b - 21_b) - (221_b - 21_b) \\
&= 2000_b - 200_b \\
&= 2b^3 - 2b^2 \\
&= 2b^2(b-1),
\end{align*}$$
which is divisible by $3$ <b><i>unless</i></b> $b\equiv2\pmod{3}.$ The only choice congruent to $2$ modulo $3$ is $\boxed{\textbf{(E)} ~8}.$
